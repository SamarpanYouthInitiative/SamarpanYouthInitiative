Empowering Students, Shaping Futures

The Samarpan Youth Initiative is dedicated to providing students with opportunities for personal and professional development through a diverse range of events and initiatives.

Professional Development

Workshops and events focused on building essential professional skills, such as resume writing, interview preparation, and networking.

Skill-Building Workshops

Interactive workshops covering a wide range of technical and non-technical skills, from coding to design to public speaking.

Community Engagement

Opportunities for students to give back to the community through volunteering, fundraising, and collaborative projects.
